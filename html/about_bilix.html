What is Bilix?

Bilix is a modern car-sharing service designed to give you all the benefits of having a car without the hassle of owning one. We believe in a future where mobility is shared, smart, and available to everyone at the touch of a button.

Simple & Digital

Everything is handled in our app. From booking to unlocking the car, your journey starts and ends with your smartphone.

Flexible Travel

Whether you need a car for an hour, a day, or a full weekend, Bilix adapts to your schedule and your needs.

Sustainable Future

By sharing cars, we reduce the number of vehicles on the road, lowering CO2 emissions and creating more space in our cities.
